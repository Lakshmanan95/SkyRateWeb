About Us
Given the complexities involved in the purchase and repair of Aircraft Parts; AviationRating.com was formed to provide its members with real-world vendor ratings and reviews from actual everyday customers who provide their experience and feedback for the vendors which are represented on this site. These ratings and reviews are meant to assist you the customer, providing you the ability in advance to have a more accurate picture of the type of company you might potentially be conducting business with; their strengths, weakness….etc., without going through the costly route of purchasing a part or sending a part in for repair having had zero advanced visibility only to find out that the vendor has terrible customer service or cannot meet their promised TAT or did not honor their quoted price. Our goal is to make it easier for all customers within the aviation sector to share and provide each other with the necessary critical information to draw upon others actual experiences in order to make a completely informed decision on who will earn your aviation business.

Since there are always two sides to every situation companies, you have the ability to Respond to reviews. Helping to make sure that Members get all the information they need in order to make a better decision on whether to use the services of your company. You will also be able to use the site to resolve and address issues that a customer may have and for your companies management team to leverage the site in order to gain a better understanding of what you are doing correctly for your customers and where you are falling short and need improvement.

All ratings are based on actual reviews by other members and are not in anyway influenced by AviationRating.com or it’s staff. What you see are the real experiences your colleagues in the industry have experienced, complaints or complements.

We ask that you join this community and share your experience. When giving a rating or review please provide a professional opinion, provide information that you would like to see when making a decision. Keep in mind that what you are saying is potentially helping someone in our industry make the correct decision and avoid the wrong decision.

All too often many of us only take the time to provide negative feedback. We ask that you please take a moment to also provide positive feedback. When a vendor does it the right way and provides you with the service you wanted let others know so that others will go to that vendor.

Thank you for being a part of this community and with the involvement of both sides customers and vendors we will be able to provide a service that will allow all of us within our industry to make the best decision as a customer and improve our services as a vendor.
